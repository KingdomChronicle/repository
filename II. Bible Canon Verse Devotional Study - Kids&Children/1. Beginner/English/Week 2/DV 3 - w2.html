The Daily Devotional Program is designed to train us to grow in Christ through God's Word.

Instruction on how to successfully kickstart your Daily Devotional Lifestyle with the Lord.

  • Start your Daily Devotion by praying sincerely to God, asking Him for understanding and guidance.
  • If you have your own Bible, open it to the verse; otherwise, read the verse from this page.
  • Next, read the devotional verse aloud three times so the enemy can hear God's Word from your lips.
  • Then, read the reflection section multiple times if needed, until you truly understand its message.
  • Then, end your devotion with prayer having a thankful heart.

Bible Verse of the Day

Jeremiah 29:11

ESV (English Standard Version)

11 For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord, plans for welfare and not for evil, to give you a future and a hope.


GUIDE REFLECTION:


Who We Are (Human Identity & Purpose)

God’s Plan for Our Lives

Have you ever wondered what your future will be like? Maybe you dream of becoming a doctor, a teacher, an artist, or an athlete. The good news is that God has a special plan for your life!

God’s Promise

The Bible says in Jeremiah 29:11,
"For I know the plans I have for you," declares the Lord, "plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future."

This means that God already knows what is best for us. His plans are full of love, hope, and blessings. Even when we face challenges, we can trust that He is guiding us.

God’s Plan Is Good

Sometimes, life doesn’t go the way we expect. We might feel sad, lost, or confused. But God is always in control. He sees the bigger picture and leads us toward what is best.

How Can We Follow God’s Plan?

  1. Pray – Talk to God and ask Him to guide you.

  2. Read the Bible – God’s Word helps us understand His plan.

  3. Trust Him – Even when things are hard, believe that God is working for your good.

  4. Do Your Best – Use your talents and work hard, knowing that God is with you.

God’s plan is always filled with love, hope, and a bright future. No matter what happens, you are never alone—God is leading the way!

Would you like to trust Him with your future today?
