  • Start your Daily Devotion by praying sincerely to God, asking Him for understanding and guidance.

Bible Verse of the Day

1 John 1:9

ESV (English Standard Version)

9 If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.

GUIDE REFLECTION:


Who We Are (Human Identity & Purpose)

Forgiveness: A Gift from God

Have you ever hurt someone by mistake, or maybe even on purpose? It happens to all of us. But here's something amazing the Bible teaches us about forgiveness. Forgiveness is when someone chooses to let go of hurt or wrongs done to them. And guess what? God offers us forgiveness too!

God's Promise of Forgiveness

In 1 John 1:9, the Bible says,
"If we confess our sins, He is faithful and just and will forgive us our sins and purify us from all unrighteousness."

This means that when we say sorry to God and admit we've done wrong, He will forgive us! God is always ready to forgive us because He loves us. He doesn't hold onto our mistakes forever. Instead, He wants to help us become better people.

Why Is Forgiveness Important?

Forgiveness is important because it helps us heal. When we forgive others, we feel better inside. It's like taking a heavy backpack off your shoulders. When we ask God to forgive us, it brings us closer to Him.

How Can We Forgive Others?

  1. Say Sorry – If you hurt someone, ask for forgiveness.

  2. Forgive Others – Even when it's hard, forgive those who hurt you.

  3. Pray – Ask God to help you forgive and to heal your heart.

Forgiveness isn't always easy, but it is always the right thing to do. When we forgive others, we become more like Jesus, who forgave us even when we didn't deserve it. Remember, God’s love is bigger than any mistake we make, and He is always ready to forgive!
