GUIDE REFLECTION:


Who We Are (Human Identity & Purpose)

Redemption: God’s Amazing Gift

Have you ever been given a gift that you didn’t expect? A gift that was so wonderful, you couldn’t believe it? Well, God offers us an even more amazing gift—redemption!

What is Redemption?

Redemption means being saved or rescued from something bad. In the Bible, it tells us that all of us have sinned and that sin separates us from God. But God loved us so much that He sent Jesus to save us!

Jesus died on the cross and came back to life, so that through Him, we can be forgiven and brought back to God. That’s redemption—God rescuing us from sin and giving us a new life.

God’s Gift of Redemption

In Ephesians 2:8-9, the Bible says,
"For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith—and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God—not by works, so that no one can boast."

This means that we don’t have to earn God’s love or forgiveness. It’s a gift that He freely gives to everyone who believes in Jesus. We are saved by grace, which is God’s unearned favor. All we have to do is believe and accept the gift of redemption!

Why Is Redemption Important?

Redemption is important because it brings us back to God. When we accept God’s gift of redemption, we are forgiven and can have a close relationship with Him. It also gives us hope, knowing that no matter what we’ve done, God can forgive and transform us.

How Can We Accept God’s Gift of Redemption?

  1. Believe in Jesus – Trust that Jesus died for your sins and rose again.

  2. Ask for Forgiveness – Admit your mistakes and ask God to forgive you.

  3. Follow Jesus – Choose to live for Him and follow His example every day.

Redemption is the most incredible gift we could ever receive. It’s not something we can earn, but something God gives out of love. Through Jesus, we can be forgiven and live a life full of purpose and hope!

Would you like to accept God’s gift of redemption today?
