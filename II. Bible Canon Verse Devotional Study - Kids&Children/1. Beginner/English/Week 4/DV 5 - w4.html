The Daily Devotional Program is designed to train us to grow in Christ through God's Word.

Instruction on how to successfully kickstart your Daily Devotional Lifestyle with the Lord.

  • Start your Daily Devotion by praying sincerely to God, asking Him for understanding and guidance.
  • If you have your own Bible, open it to the verse; otherwise, read the verse from this page.
  • Next, read the devotional verse aloud three times so the enemy can hear God's Word from your lips.
  • Then, read the reflection section multiple times if needed, until you truly understand its message.
  • Then, end your devotion with prayer having a thankful heart.

Bible Verse of the Day

John 13:34-35

ESV (English Standard Version)

34 A new commandment I give to you, that you love one another: just as I have loved you, you also are to love one another. 35 By this all people will know that you are my disciples, if you have love for one another.


GUIDE REFLECTION:


Living for God (Christian Discipleship)

Love Others Like Jesus!

Have you ever felt really loved by someone? Maybe your parents give you warm hugs, your friends cheer you up when you're sad, or your teacher helps you with a hard lesson. Love is one of the most wonderful gifts we can give and receive.

Jesus showed us what true love looks like. In John 13:34-35, He said, “A new command I give you: Love one another. As I have loved you, so you must love one another. By this everyone will know that you are my disciples, if you love one another.”

Jesus didn’t just talk about love—He showed it through His actions. He was kind to the poor, patient with His friends, forgiving to those who hurt Him, and He even gave His life for us! That’s the kind of love He wants us to show to others.

So, how can we love like Jesus?

  • Be kind – Even a smile, a kind word, or sharing your things shows love.

  • Forgive others – When someone hurts you, don't stay angry. Jesus forgave us, and we can forgive too.

  • Help those in need – Whether it’s helping at home or sharing lunch with someone who forgot theirs, love finds ways to help.

  • Include others – Invite someone who’s alone to join your game or group. Jesus always made people feel welcome.

When we love like Jesus, other people will notice! They’ll see there’s something special about us—and that special thing is Jesus living in our hearts.

So let’s make it our mission every day to love others the way Jesus loves us. It might not always be easy, but it will always be worth it.

Let’s be known by our love!
