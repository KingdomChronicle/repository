Daily Devotional Verses

The Daily Devotional Program is designed to train us to grow in Christ through God's Word.

Instruction on how to successfully kickstart your Daily Devotional Lifestyle with the Lord.

  • Start your Daily Devotion by praying sincerely to God, asking Him for understanding and guidance.
  • If you have your own Bible, open it to the verse; otherwise, read the verse from this page.
  • Next, read the devotional verse aloud three times so the enemy can hear God's Word from your lips.
  • Then, read the reflection section multiple times if needed, until you truly understand its message.
  • Then, end your devotion with prayer having a thankful heart.

Bible Verse of the Day



Deuteronomy 31:6

ESV (English Standard Version)

6Be strong and courageous. Do not fear or be in dread of them, for it is the Lord your God who goes with you. He will not leave you or forsake you.


GUIDE REFLECTION:


Who God Is (The Nature of God)

God Is Always Present

This verse reminds us that God is always with us. No matter where we go or what happens in our lives, He never leaves our side. Sometimes, we may feel alone or scared, especially when facing problems or challenges. But God promises that we don’t have to be afraid because He is always there to help us.

Think about how a loving parent or a good friend stays beside you when you need comfort. God is even greater than that—He is with us every second of our lives. Whether we are happy or sad, strong or weak, He watches over us and gives us strength.

Because God is always present, we can talk to Him anytime through prayer. We don’t have to wait for a special moment—He listens to us wherever we are. When we trust in Him, we will feel safe and at peace, knowing that He will never abandon us.

As children of God, we can be strong and courageous, just as the Bible says. No matter what happens, we can move forward with confidence because God walks with us every step of the way.
