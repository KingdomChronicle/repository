Proverbs 8:17

NIV (New International Version)

I love those who love me, and those who seek me find me.

GUIDE REFLECTION:

Wisdom Found by Those Who Truly Seek It

Wisdom Is for Those Who Truly Seek It

In this verse, wisdom speaks like a person giving a promise:

👉 “If you diligently seek me, you will surely find me.”

This shows that God’s wisdom is not only for a chosen few. It is open to everyone who is willing to:

  • seek
  • listen
  • humble themselves
  • and obey God

But wisdom is not found by those who have no interest or desire to know God.

True Love for Wisdom

The “love” mentioned here is not merely an emotion.

It is shown through:

  • valuing wisdom
  • obeying it
  • and choosing God’s wisdom daily

👉 A person who truly loves wisdom desires to live according to it.

When we choose to live by God’s wisdom:

  • our path becomes straighter
  • we are protected from destruction
  • and our relationship with the Lord grows deeper

The Love of Christ

The greatest example of love is found in Jesus Christ.

He did not only love us in words—He demonstrated His love through His sacrifice on the cross.

Epistle to the Galatians says:

“The Son of God loved me and gave himself for me.”

👉 Jesus’ love is the reason we learn to love Him.

As First Epistle of John says:

“We love because He first loved us.”

God Is Found by Those Who Seek Him Sincerely

God also gives this promise in Book of Jeremiah:

“You will seek me and find me when you seek me with all your heart.”

God does not hide Himself from those who sincerely come to Him.

👉 The problem is not that God is distant, but that many people are not truly seeking Him.

Many people spend their lives seeking:

  • money
  • fame
  • pleasure
  • and success

But only a few seriously seek the wisdom and presence of God.

Seeking with Diligence

Seeking wisdom requires:

  • time
  • effort
  • prayer
  • and continual devotion to the Word of God

A single moment of listening is not enough.

👉 A person who hungers for God’s wisdom continues to seek Him daily.

Like someone searching for a priceless treasure, believers should also pursue God with determination.

Personal Application: What Are You Diligently Seeking?

Examine yourself:

  • What do I spend most of my time seeking?
  • Am I more determined to succeed in the world than to grow in God?
  • Do I make time to seek God through prayer and His Word?
  • Is my love for God genuine, or only words?

What you consistently devote your time to will eventually fill your heart.

Conclusion

God’s wisdom is not hidden from those who sincerely seek it.

👉 God is willing to reveal His wisdom to hearts that love Him and faithfully draw near to Him.

The more we seek Him, the more we come to know His:

  • truth
  • love
  • and guidance for life

The question is:

What are you truly seeking every day—temporary things, or the eternal wisdom and presence of God?
