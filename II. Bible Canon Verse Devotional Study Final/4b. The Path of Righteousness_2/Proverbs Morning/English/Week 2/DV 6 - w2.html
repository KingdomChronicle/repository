Proverbs 8:10

NIV (New International Version)

10Choose my instruction instead of silver, knowledge rather than choice gold,

GUIDE REFLECTION:

The True Value of Wisdom

Two Choices: Wealth or Wisdom

In this verse, wisdom presents a clear challenge:

👉 What will you value more—gold or wisdom?

It does not say that silver or gold are evil. Rather, it reveals that something holds greater worth.

  • Silver and gold → temporary
  • Wisdom → eternal in value

The Limitations of Wealth

In the world, wealth is often seen as the key to success and happiness.

But the truth is:

  • It cannot buy true peace
  • It cannot save the soul
  • It cannot be taken into the life to come

Even the richest person:

👉 Will die and leave everything behind

Moreover, wealth can become a danger:

  • It brings temptation
  • It can distance a person from God
  • It may lead to destruction

“For we brought nothing into the world, and we can take nothing out of it.” (1 Timothy 6:7)

The Wealth That Cannot Be Bought

Wisdom offers what money cannot purchase:

  • Right direction in life
  • Knowledge of truth
  • Firm moral grounding
  • Relationship with God

👉 There are things money cannot buy—and this is where the true value of wisdom is found.

A Warning from Jesus

In Jesus’ account of the rich man (Luke 16:19–31), we see:

  • He was rich on earth
  • But spiritually poor

In the end, his wealth could not help him in the life to come.

👉 The lesson: Material wealth is useless without the wisdom of God.

The Value of the Word of God

King David understood this truth:

“The commands of the Lord… are more precious than gold.” (Psalm 19:9–10)

The Word of God is:

  • True
  • Righteous
  • More valuable than the greatest earthly treasure

Application: What Do You Value Most?

The real question is not whether you have money—but what matters most to you.

Examine yourself:

  • Do I prioritize income over truth?
  • Do I spend more time pursuing wealth than growing in God?
  • Am I willing to trade wisdom for temporary gain?

What you value will determine the direction of your life.

Conclusion

Wisdom gives a clear call:

👉 Value it more than silver and gold.

For wealth will pass away—but wisdom carries eternal value.

The question is: What do you desire more—riches that fade, or wisdom that endures forever?

The choice you make today will shape your future—even into eternity.
