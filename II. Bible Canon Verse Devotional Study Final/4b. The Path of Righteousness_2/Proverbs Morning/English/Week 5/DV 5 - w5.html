Proverbs 8:30

NIV (New International Version)

Then I was constantly at his side. I was filled with delight day after day, rejoicing always in his presence,

GUIDE REFLECTION:

Wisdom Was with God

Wisdom Was Present in God’s Work

In this verse, wisdom is described as always being with God in His work.

👉 This shows that everything God does has purpose, order, and perfect wisdom.

The world was not created randomly or by mere chance.

Everything was made:

  • with design
  • with order
  • with purpose
  • and with wisdom

From the vastness of the heavens to the smallest details of life, God’s wisdom can be seen everywhere.

God Does Nothing Without Purpose

Sometimes events in our lives seem meaningless.

There are moments when we ask:

  • “Why did this happen?”
  • “What is the purpose of this?”
  • “Why did God allow this?”

But this verse reminds us:

👉 God never works without purpose.

If God had wisdom in creating the entire universe, then He also has wisdom in shaping our lives.

We may not see the whole picture now, but He sees the beginning and the end.

The Close Relationship Between God and Wisdom

The verse describes wisdom as:

  • a companion
  • a helper
  • and a source of delight in God’s work

This reveals the close relationship between God and His wisdom.

In the New Testament, Jesus Christ is also called the Word of God.

According to Gospel of John:

“In the beginning was the Word... and through Him all things were made.”

While Proverbs uses poetic imagery and personification, it also reveals the truth that all of God’s works are filled with wisdom.

👉 God never makes mistakes in His plans.

Wisdom as a Guide for Daily Life

If God uses wisdom in everything He does, how much more should we?

We make decisions every day about:

  • family
  • work
  • education
  • relationships
  • and the future

Yet many people act first before seeking God’s guidance.

👉 A wise person learns to seek direction from the Lord first.

It is not enough to rely on:

  • personal experience
  • emotions
  • or the opinions of others

True guidance comes from God.

Personal Application: Who Guides You?

Examine yourself:

  • Do I ask God for wisdom before making decisions?
  • Do I rely on my own understanding?
  • Do I trust that God has a purpose for my life?
  • Do I seek God’s will daily?

The person who seeks God’s guidance gains greater direction and peace.

Conclusion

Wisdom was always with God in His work.

👉 If God uses perfect wisdom in everything He does, we can also trust His guidance in our lives.

Even if we do not immediately understand everything that happens, we can be certain that God has a good purpose behind every situation and event in our lives.

The question:

When making decisions, do you follow your own wisdom—or the guidance of God?
