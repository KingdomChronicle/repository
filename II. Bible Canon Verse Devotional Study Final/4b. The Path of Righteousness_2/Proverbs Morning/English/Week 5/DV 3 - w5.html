Proverbs 8:26-27

NIV (New International Version)

26 before he made the world or its fields or any of the dust of the earth.27 I was there when he set the heavens in place, when he marked out the horizon on the face of the deep,

GUIDE REFLECTION:

God Is the Beginning of Everything

Everything Has an Origin

In these verses, it is once again shown that wisdom was already there before God created:

  • the earth
  • the fields
  • the dust of the world
  • and even the heavens

👉 Before anything we see today existed, God and His wisdom were already there.

Today, many people ask:

  • “Where did everything come from?”
  • “How did life begin?”
  • “What is the origin of the world?”

The Bible gives a clear answer:

👉 God is the beginning of everything.

Everything that exists did not appear without purpose or simply happen by chance.

All things are the result of God’s perfect wisdom and power.

Human Beings Are Not the True Source

People often think that because of knowledge and technology, humanity can control everything.

We can create:

  • buildings
  • vehicles
  • advanced technology
  • and many inventions

But there is one important truth:

👉 Human beings cannot create the original source of all things.

We may use materials that already exist, but only God created them out of nothing.

We may plant seeds in the ground, but God created:

  • the earth
  • the seed
  • the rain
  • and life itself

Human wisdom has limits, but God’s wisdom is eternal.

The Heavens Declare the Glory of God

When we look up at:

  • the clouds
  • the stars
  • the sun
  • and the vast sky

we are often amazed by their beauty.

But all these things carry a deeper message:

👉 There is a Great Creator behind everything.

Book of Psalms says:

“The heavens declare the glory of God.”

Creation should never become the object of our worship.

Instead, it should lead us to the God who created everything.

Christ Is the Beginning and Center of Everything

Jesus Christ is the complete expression of God’s wisdom.

According to Gospel of John:

“Through Him all things were made; without Him nothing was made that has been made.”

👉 Christ is not merely part of history; He was present at the beginning of everything.

If He had the wisdom to create the entire universe, then He certainly has the wisdom to guide our lives.

The Foundation of Every Plan

Many people make plans for:

  • work
  • family
  • business
  • or the future

But often they forget to include God.

👉 Any plan without God as its foundation can easily fall apart.

When God is at the center of our plans:

  • we gain right direction
  • peace
  • and wisdom for every step

Personal Application: Who Is the Foundation of Your Life?

Examine yourself:

  • Do I include God in my plans?
  • Do I trust my own abilities more than God?
  • Do I acknowledge God as the source of everything I have?
  • Are my decisions based on His wisdom?

The foundation of your life determines its direction.

Conclusion

God is the beginning of everything, and His wisdom existed before the world was created.

👉 If God is the foundation of all creation, then He should also be the foundation of our lives.

The world may change and human plans may fail, but God’s wisdom remains firm forever.

The question:

Who is the foundation of your plans today—your own understanding, or God, the Creator of all things?
