Proverbs 8:24-25

NIV (New International Version)

24 When there were no watery depths, I was given birth, when there were no springs overflowing with water;25 before the mountains were settled in place, before the hills, I was given birth,

GUIDE REFLECTION:

Wisdom Existed Before Anything Was Created

Wisdom Is Greater Than Anything Created

In these verses, it is again shown that wisdom already existed before God created:

  • the seas
  • springs of water
  • hills
  • and mountains

These things represent:

  • strength
  • stability
  • and greatness

To human eyes, mountains symbolize firmness and permanence, while the vast sea represents power.

But wisdom says:

👉 Before all these things existed, God’s wisdom was already there.

This reminds us that nothing in creation is greater or higher than God’s wisdom.

People Often Depend on Their Own Strength

Human nature often leads us to trust in:

  • our own abilities
  • experience
  • knowledge
  • or material things

Sometimes we think:

  • “I can handle this.”
  • “I am capable enough.”
  • “I have enough strength to face this.”

But life quickly reminds us that human strength has limits.

👉 Human strength may fail, but God’s wisdom never falls short.

Look to the Creator, Not the Creation

When we see towering mountains and vast oceans, it is natural to be amazed.

But our admiration should not stop there.

Book of Psalms says:

“I lift up my eyes to the mountains—where does my help come from? My help comes from the Lord, the Maker of heaven and earth.”

👉 Our hope should not rest in created things but in the God who created them all.

Many people admire the beauty and power of the world while forgetting its Creator.

True wisdom teaches us to recognize God behind everything He has made.

Christ Is the True Wisdom

Jesus Christ is the perfect expression of God’s wisdom.

Through Him:

  • all things were created
  • all things continue to hold together
  • and in Him we find direction for life

👉 Wisdom is not merely knowledge; it is a relationship with God through Christ.

When Christ is with us, we have guidance greater than any human ability.

Personal Application: Where Do You Place Your Trust?

Examine yourself:

  • Do I rely more on my own strength than on God?
  • Do I ask for wisdom before making decisions?
  • When problems come, do I immediately depend on my own abilities?
  • Do I remember that God is the source of true wisdom?

A person who depends only on themselves can easily become discouraged, but the person who depends on God builds a strong foundation.

Conclusion

Before there were seas and mountains, God’s wisdom already existed.

👉 This means that God’s wisdom is stronger and greater than any power or strength in this world.

If God had the wisdom to create the entire universe, then He surely has the wisdom to guide your life.

The question is:

Who do you trust more right now—your own strength, or God’s eternal wisdom?
