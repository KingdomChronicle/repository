Proverbs 8:22-23

NIV (New International Version)

22 “The Lord brought me forth as the first of his works, before his deeds of old;23 I was formed long ages ago, at the very beginning, when the world came to be.

GUIDE REFLECTION:

Wisdom Is Part of God’s Eternal Plan

Wisdom Existed Before Everything Else

In these verses, wisdom is described as already existing before the world was created.

👉 Before there were:

  • earth
  • seas
  • mountains
  • stars
  • or humanity

God’s wisdom was already there.

Wisdom did not begin with:

  • humanity
  • schools
  • science
  • governments
  • or philosophy

True wisdom comes from God Himself.

It is part of His eternal plan.

👉 God never acts without purpose or direction.

Everything He created came from His perfect wisdom.

Creation Reveals God’s Wisdom

When we look at nature and all creation, we can see the amazing wisdom of God.

This can be seen in:

  • the precise movement of the stars
  • the order of nature
  • the changing of seasons
  • the life of animals
  • and the complexity of human beings

Nothing happened by accident.

👉 God is a God of order, not confusion.

Everything in creation points to a powerful and wise Creator.

Christ: The Perfect Expression of Wisdom

God’s wisdom was fully revealed in Jesus Christ.

Epistle to the Colossians says:

“In Christ are hidden all the treasures of wisdom and knowledge.”

And according to Gospel of John:

“Through Him all things were made.”

👉 Jesus is not only a teacher of wisdom; He Himself is the perfect expression of it.

In Him we find:

  • truth
  • direction
  • and purpose for life

God’s Plan Is Greater Than Our Understanding

There are times when we do not understand what is happening in our lives.

Sometimes we ask:

  • “Why is this happening?”
  • “Why is there suffering?”
  • “Why does there seem to be no clear direction?”

But these verses remind us:

👉 The God who created the whole world with wisdom also has a plan for our lives.

We may not see the full picture now, but God sees the beginning and the end.

What we do not understand today is still part of His perfect purpose.

Wisdom Is a Gift from God

Although wisdom comes from God, He also desires to share it with His children.

Those who come to Christ are given:

  • guidance
  • understanding
  • and wisdom for daily living

👉 We do not need to rely only on our own understanding.

We have a God who is willing to guide us.

Personal Application: Do You Trust God’s Plan?

Examine yourself:

  • Are there things I am trying to understand only through my own ability?
  • Do I trust God’s plan even when I do not fully understand it?
  • Do I ask the Lord for wisdom before making decisions?
  • Do I believe that God has a purpose for my life?

Trusting God begins with recognizing that His wisdom is greater than our own understanding.

Conclusion

Wisdom did not begin with humanity—it is part of God’s eternal plan.

👉 Before the world was created, God’s wisdom already existed and continues to work even today.

If God had the wisdom to create the entire universe, then He surely has a good plan for your life.

The question is:

Will you trust your own understanding, or God’s eternal wisdom?
