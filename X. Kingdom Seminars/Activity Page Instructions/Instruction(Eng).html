The First Step on Your Journey



As a new participant in this noble mission, it is important for you to understand that the programs you will join and the daily tasks you will undertake are part of a much deeper process—one that is meant to shape your character and prepare you to stand strong, act with conviction, and succeed in advancing the cause we share for our nation.

You will begin with the foundational steps. Each week, you will receive an upgrade—a new level of learning and personal development. As you continue to grow in understanding, reflection, and action, you will gradually form the qualities of a modern and purpose-driven Filipino hero—one who is knowledgeable, servant-hearted, and courageous enough to rise for the country.

In the first week, you will be assigned to write one reflection each day. In the second week, you will write two—one in the morning and another in the evening.

This system will be explained in full during your first attendance at the Online Kapehan, where we will also explore the overall direction, purpose, and spiritual foundation of the program.

As we saw in the final video, you can only carry out your role effectively when your heart is pure and filled with light. This daily journey is not merely about reading and writing reflections—it is a process of shaping your courage, convictions, and inner holiness, so that your entire being may be molded into a Filipino who is truly patriotic, God-fearing, and compassionate toward others.

This is a vital part of our collective movement forward. Through this process, we elevate the standard of every individual—in thought, in heart, and in purpose. In doing so, we close the gaps that may otherwise lead to the collapse or failure of this mission. That is why we ensure that every modern hero who will rise for the nation possesses a pure heart, a good spirit, and a firm faith in God as we pursue our shared dream: to lift the Philippines and bring it back to its true greatness.

Because the true battle for national restoration cannot be won by strength or intellect alone, but through a pure heart and the help of God, who gives light and strength to those who are willing to serve faithfully.

Weekly Instructions for Your Reflections

  1. Monday

    Read the message sent for Monday. If there is an accompanying video, watch it first before writing your reflection. Write your reflection based on the message and/or video. Submit your reflection in The Filipino Chronicles group chat anytime during the day. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully to complete the reflection properly.

  2. Tuesday

    Read the message for Tuesday. Write a reflection about it. Submit it in the group chat on the same day.

  3. Wednesday to Saturday

    Read the message for each day. Write a reflection and submit it to the group chat.

  4. Sunday

    No activity message for the day.

    Attend the online kapehan (coffee gathering) as part of the discussion at 8:00 pm:

    Share your learnings from the reflections throughout the week.

    Listen to the experiences and insights of other Filipinos in the program.

Guide each participant to explore their individual pages and group chats. It is important for every participant to learn how to use and navigate their program page and the designated group chat.
