Book Possible Timeline Notes
Tobit ~8th–7th century BC (setting); written around 200–100 BC Story set during Assyrian exile (8th century BC), but likely written much later.
Judith ~6th–2nd century BC (setting unclear); written ~150 BC Story set in vague post-exile time, likely fictionalized history.
Ester (Greek additions) Original story ~5th century BC; Greek additions ~2nd century BC Additions to the Hebrew Esther, emphasizing God's role.
1 Macabeo Covers 175–134 BC; written ~100 BC History of Jewish revolt against Seleucid Empire.
2 Macabeo Covers 180–160 BC; written ~124 BC Focuses on religious aspects of Maccabean revolt; a summary of a larger work.
Karunungan ni Solomon (Wisdom of Solomon) Written ~50–30 BC Philosophical reflections, likely composed in Greek-speaking Jewish community in Egypt.
Ecclesiastico (Sirach) Written ~180–175 BC Traditional Jewish wisdom literature, authored by Jesus Ben Sirach.
Baruc Set ~6th century BC (exile); written ~200–100 BC Attributed to Baruch, Jeremiah’s scribe; reflects later Jewish exile concerns.
Liham ni Jeremias Set 6th century BC; written ~300–100 BC A warning against idolatry, connected to exile period.
Awit ng Tatlong Kabataan Setting ~6th century BC (Babylonian exile); added ~2nd–1st century BC Expansion of the Book of Daniel, added in Greek.
Susana Set ~6th century BC (Babylonian exile); added ~2nd–1st century BC Story inserted into Daniel in Greek tradition.
Si Bel at ang Dragon Set ~6th century BC (Babylonian exile); added ~2nd–1st century BC Another addition to Daniel, about defeating pagan gods.
