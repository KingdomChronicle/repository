INVITATION TO THE NATION’S PRAYER INTERCESSION


Good day, servant of the Lord!


As one of the Chosen in the Kingdom of God Movement, and as someone called to minister and proclaim His Word, we warmly invite you to be part of our Weekly Online Prayer Meeting and Intercession every Monday at 7:30 PM.

This sacred gathering is more than just a time of prayer — it is a divine appointment where we unite in spirit to intercede for our nation and to declare God's will over the land. Together, we stand as watchmen on the wall, lifting up the Philippines before the throne of grace.

We encourage your consistent participation over the course of one month. After this, we would be honored to invite you as one of the prayer leaders, helping to strengthen this intercessory ministry as we contend for revival and righteousness in our nation.

We look forward to praying alongside you — for His glory and the advancement of His Kingdom on earth.


See you on Monday!

In Christ,
