"Me, Myself, and My Beloved Country" is a 24-day challenge to know and love both myself and my country. It is a personal growth journey designed to help us discover our true identity—as God’s masterpiece and as proud Filipinos. This program aims to deepen our understanding of who we are, nurture a strong sense of national identity, and awaken within us a genuine love and care for our homeland. Through daily reflection, we are trained to grow spiritually, emotionally, and patriotically—for the glory of God and the good of our nation.


Guidelines for Understanding and Living Out my Reflections:

  • Read with both heart and mind. Take time to read the message slowly and thoughtfully. Feel every word, and seek to understand the author’s intent. What truth is being revealed to you?
  • Identify the central message. What is the main theme of the message? What reality is it trying to awaken us to? Recognize the call to action—and to whom it is being addressed.
  • Reflect on the historical context. Use the historical references in the message as a mirror to examine our nation’s present state. What aspects of our society have remained the same? What lessons can we learn?
  • Recognize the emotions expressed. Feel the sorrow, frustration, hope, and urgency conveyed in each paragraph. This is not just literary content—it is the cry of a patriotic heart.
  • Write your personal reflection. What struck you the most? Was there a part of the message that deeply resonated with you? Write a short reflective paragraph expressing your thoughts and feelings and share it on our community chat.
  • Take concrete actions to show love for the nation. What can you do today in response to the message’s call? Start small—with honesty, respect, discipline, and helping others. True patriotism is measured by action.
  • Keep the spirit of the message alive in daily life. Don’t let the message end with reading. Let it live on in your thoughts, your words, and most importantly, your actions.

“Awakening: A Call to a Nation That Has Forgotten Itself”


There are times when silence is no longer humility—but betrayal. When stillness is not a sign of peace, but of forgetting.
And forgetting becomes the quiet surrender of our dignity.

This is that time.
This is the moment to awaken—not just to see the light,
but to remember who we truly are.

In the midst of noise and lies, in the echo of collective amnesia, our identity has been slowly erased.
Like waves crashing endlessly upon the shore, time has swallowed the memory of our origins.

But now, a new wave rises—
not to destroy, but to rebuild.
Not to deceive, but to reveal the truth.

For far too long, the true face of the Philippines has been hidden.
What the world has seen is a portrait crafted by those who enslaved and betrayed us—a picture full of weakness and hopelessness.
We have accepted that image as reality, until we forgot:
That is not who we are.

But here is the truth:

We are not cursed—we are chosen.
We are not abandoned—we are destined.
And we are not defeated—we are destined to rise and lead.

We are a nation forged in fire, tested by history, and sealed with divine purpose. Our story is not finished—this is just the true beginning—the start of the greatest chapter in our history.

And so this is a call—
Not merely to stand, but to return to our roots, to awaken the memory of our ancestors, and to restore the dignity that was once stolen from us.

We have not lost our ability—we have lost our memory.

And with the loss of memory, the nation has lost its way. It no longer understands the true value of its actions:

We celebrate heroes, yet forget the values they fought for.

We wave our flag, yet feel ashamed of who we are.

We build churches, yet our conscience, spirit, and soul remain asleep.

We cry for justice in the streets and on social media, yet neglect integrity in private life.

Now is the time to change the narrative.

If some are still asleep—let us wake them.
If shame still lingers—let us restore our honor.
If fear grips us—let us replace it with courage.
And if silence prevails—let us raise the voice of truth.

Because we are not Filipinos by blood alone—
We are Filipinos by destiny.

Like Nehemiah, we are the generation called to rebuild our broken walls—
Not driven by anger, but by purpose.
Not for selfish gain, but for the future of our nation.

“But you are a chosen people, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, God’s special possession...” — 1 Peter 2:9

So we rise—not because it is easy, but because it is right.
We fight—not out of hate, but out of hope.
We rebuild—not as perfect, but as chosen bearers of goodness.

And I say this with full conviction:
There are still heroes in this land.
And you—you are one of them.

Conclusion

But what good is awakening if we remain bound by inaction?
What use is light if we still choose to live in shadows?
What is the value of truth if it leads to no action?

The truth has already emerged—it can no longer be denied.
The heavens reveal our true identity—it can no longer be postponed.
And the call of this era is no longer a whisper—it is a drumbeat pounding in our hearts, urging us to walk, to move, to rise and rebuild our nation.

Now more than ever, each one of us must ask:
What is my response to my nation’s call?

Observing is no longer enough. Shouting on social media is no longer enough.
This is the time for bold action—the time to take a stand.

Stand up, my fellow Filipino.
Not for yourself, but for the sake of our people.
Not to hide our wounds, but to prove that we were forged through them.

You are part of this story.
You are the answer to the prayers of our ancestors.
And you are the hope of the generations to come.

Begin to fully own your identity as a Filipino—not half, but whole.
Continue with courage—do not look back, for the future is waiting.

And now, together, let us break the chains of falsehood that have long bound our nation.

You are chosen—not to watch, but to lead.
You are called—not to remain silent, but to speak.
You are the hope—not of the few, but of the entire nation.

And if we respond together, as one people—
The rebirth of our nation will no longer be a dream.
It will become reality—
A fire rekindled in the heart of every Filipino people.

Heaven awaits our answer.
This is the moment.
Let us rise. Let us act.
And together, let us awaken a nation that once forgot—
But now, at last, begins to remember.
